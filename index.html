our service

Our List Of Services

Dental treatment Painless and comfortable treatment using computer anesthesia, modern technologies and highly aesthetic materials. Possible treatment in sedation (medicated sleep).
Dentistry Surgery Using gentle, high-tech techniques and the best equipment for optimal results. Painless surgical procedures of any complexity. Maximum comfort for the patient during treatment.
Dentistry Prosthetics Complete restoration of the chewing function and aesthetics of the patient's smile, regardless of his age, in any clinical situation. High-quality replacement of extracted or lost teeth.
Orthodontics Correction of bite, alignment of teeth at any age. Modern leveling systems manufactured in Germany and America, the use of virtual planning and 3D modeling.
Children's Dentistry Experienced professionals who easily find an approach to children. Treatment using computer anesthesia and sedation. Toys, coloring books and cartoons for a comfortable stay of your baby at the reception.
Periodontal Center Application of a professional technique for evaluating periodontal tissues using a PSR screening card. Medical treatment and sterilization of periodontal pockets with a laser.
